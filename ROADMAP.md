# JustVisiting — Roadmap

## Discovery & Gamification

- **Achievements / milestones** — unlock badges for reaching thresholds ("Visited 100 towns", "All cities in Yorkshire"). The app already tracks everything needed.
- **Nearby unvisited list** — a distance-sorted list of unvisited places close to the user's current location, giving a goal while out driving.

## Data & Export

- **Export visit history** — export visited places as CSV or GPX for analysis in other tools.
- **Share session summary** — generate a shareable image after a session (X places visited, Y miles traveled).

## Map

- **Fog of war overlay** — shade unexplored regions and reveal them as the user visits places.
- **Tap cluster to zoom/filter** — tapping a cluster bubble zooms into that area or filters to its contents rather than just showing a count.

## Sessions

- **Name / tag sessions** — let users give a session a custom name or tag (e.g. "Road trip to Scotland").
- **Session stats** — show distance traveled, total time, and places-per-hour for each session.

## Widgets & Live Activity

- **Home screen widget** — show today's visit count or current session status from the home screen.
- **Live Activity** — display a session in progress on the Dynamic Island / Lock Screen.
